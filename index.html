01 clipping_mask/

Clipping Mask

Using clipping masks to confine artwork, textures, or adjustments within the shape of a layer below. A powerful non-destructive technique that keeps designs clean and flexible while adding depth and creativity.

  • Tools: Photoshop
  • Skills: Clipping masks, non-destructive editing, layer management
02 layer_mask/

Layer Mask

Harnessing the power of layer masks to reveal or hide parts of a layer non-destructively. Black hides, white reveals — a fundamental technique for seamless blending, compositing, and precise edge control.

  • Tools: Photoshop
  • Skills: Layer masks, non-destructive editing, compositing
